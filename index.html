Seth Poulsen

Hi, I'm Seth! I create and evaluate software tools for computer science education.

Much of my research focuses on teaching algorithms and computing theory, including using Proof Blocks, a tool which I created to help students learn to write mathematical proofs. I'm also involved in various educational data analysis projects.

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computing at Utah State University.

Previously I was a Ph.D. student at University of Illinois Computer Science, and before that I was a Software Engineer at Amazon.com, working on Kindle Web Rendering and the Kindle Lite Android app.
